Background

In November 2015 while working as a nonprofit consultant I suffered a severe spinal cord injury that has left me paraplegic after a year and 1/2 in the hospital.

I moved to Rockport Texas in April 2017 to continue my recovery and to care for my disabled 80 year old father who is on dialysis.

4 months later. tragedy struck again in the form of Hurricane Harvey, destroying my newly adopted home town and our recently retrofitted wheelchair friendly home.

With the help of friends we were evacuated safely to a hotel where we'll need to stay while our destroyed house is rebuilt (dad is recovering in the hospital with heart issues but OK ).

The silver lining is that I can now use my nonprofit background and work as Grants Manager for the Red Cross September 11 Recovery Program and as Director of Disaster Preparedness for the Red Cross of Greater New York to insure your donations will go to a soon to be formed 501(c)(3) nonprofit to provide assistance in rebuilding the devastation in Rockport and along the Gulf Coast of Texas.

Thank y'all for your kind thoughts and generous nature.
